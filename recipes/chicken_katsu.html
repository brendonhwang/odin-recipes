Chicken Katsu

Description

Chicken Katsu is a delicious Japanese dish where chicken is breaded and deep-fried, resulting in a crispy golden exterior and tender, juicy meat on the inside. Itâ€™s often served with a tangy tonkatsu sauce and rice.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 2 cups panko breadcrumbs
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • Tonkatsu sauce for serving
  • Rice for serving

Steps

  1. Preheat the oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  2. Dredge the chicken breasts in flour, then dip them in the beaten eggs, and coat them in panko breadcrumbs.
  3. Fry the chicken breasts for 3-4 minutes per side until golden brown and cooked through.
  4. Drain excess oil and serve with tonkatsu sauce and rice.
