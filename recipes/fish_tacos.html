Fish Tacos

Description

Fish tacos are a fresh and delicious option for seafood lovers, made with crispy fish fillets, creamy slaw, and a zesty lime sauce, all wrapped in a soft tortilla. Perfect for a quick and tasty meal!

Ingredients

  • 4 fish fillets (like cod or tilapia)
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup shredded cabbage
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 1 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • 8 small tortillas

Steps

  1. Season fish fillets with paprika, garlic powder, cumin, and salt.
  2. Coat the fillets with flour and pan-fry them in oil for 3-4 minutes per side until crispy.
  3. Mix the shredded cabbage, sour cream, mayonnaise, and lime juice to make the slaw.
  4. Assemble the tacos by placing the fish fillets in tortillas, topping with the slaw, and serving with lime wedges.
