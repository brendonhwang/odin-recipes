Description

This World's Best Lasagna is a hearty, comforting dish packed with layers of rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta cheese, mozzarella, and Parmesan. This lasagna is a crowd favorite and is perfect for any family meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb sweet Italian sausage
  • 3/4 lb lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 (28 ounces) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6 ounces) cans tomato paste
  • 2 (6.5 ounces) cans tomato sauce
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 1 teaspoon fennel seeds
  • 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
  • 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 4 tablespoons chopped fresh basil
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 lb mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large pan, cook sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat.
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, and water. Season with sugar, basil, fennel, Italian seasoning, parsley, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 30 minutes.
  4. Meanwhile, bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook lasagna noodles until al dente, about 8 minutes. Drain, and rinse with cold water.
  5. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, and 3/4 teaspoon salt.
  6. Spread 1/3 of the meat sauce in a 9x13-inch baking dish. Top with a layer of lasagna noodles, followed by 1/3 of the ricotta mixture, 1/3 of the mozzarella, and 1/3 of the Parmesan. Repeat layering, finishing with the remaining meat sauce on top.
  7. Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 45 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10 minutes until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
  8. Let lasagna cool for about 15 minutes before slicing and serving.
