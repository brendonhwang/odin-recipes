Salmon Sushi Bake

Description

Salmon sushi bake combines the flavors of sushi into a hot, baked casserole. This dish includes seasoned rice, creamy salmon, and a crispy topping—perfect for a sushi-inspired dinner!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sushi rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 lb salmon fillets
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tbsp sriracha sauce
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 sheet nori (seaweed)
  • Sesame seeds for garnish

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the sushi rice according to the package instructions and set aside.
  3. Place the salmon fillets on a baking sheet and bake for 15-20 minutes until fully cooked. Shred the salmon into bite-sized pieces.
  4. Mix mayonnaise, sriracha, soy sauce, rice vinegar, and sugar in a bowl. Add the shredded salmon to the mixture and stir to combine.
  5. Layer the cooked sushi rice in a baking dish and top with the salmon mixture.
  6. Bake for 10 minutes until hot and bubbly. Garnish with sesame seeds and nori before serving.
